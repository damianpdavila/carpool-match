How Does It Work?

It's easy, just 1, 2, 3:

Choose from up to 10 preference criteria.

You choose exactly what's important to you.

We match your preferences against everyone in our database and find the best possible matches.

You'll see the list of potential matches, plus see them on a map to help you narrow your choice.

You and the match(es) then finalize the car pool directly: what days, who drives, what time, etc.

You contact your match(es) directly in the site. Best of all, the recipient's real name, address, and email are not visible. The recipient privacy is protected.

And so is yours!

About Us

"Your daughter goes to school where?!"

The inspiration for this car pool matching app sprung directly from driving my daughters to school. Google says the distance to St. Thomas Aquinas High School is 27.5 miles each way, twice a day, 5 days per week or 550 miles per week. Ugh. For 8 years. Double ugh.

Then there was that one glorious year [the sounds of angel choirs singing] that the Car Pool Happened.

This app should have been built then, but life. One day, in the last semester of my youngest daughter's senior year, the opportunity arose to build this app and there you go.

Frequently Asked Questions

"No, you do not swipe right."

  • How does this work?

    Easy, just specify your preferences and Co-Muter will immediately match you up!

    In case you missed it, there's a whole section above with more details.

  • What if I don't want to drive 10 miles to the dropoff

    No problem, just set your preferences with how far you are willing to drive for the dropoff.

    And same thing if you don't want to carpool with a smoker, or can only drop off at school not pick up, etc. You have up to 10 preferences you can set to make the perfect Carpool Match!

  • Sounds cool, but I don't wanna broadcast my personal info to the world

    We get you. Your name, email, preferences -- everything -- is not visible. All communications are done through Co-muter so you only share what you want to share.

  • Why don't I get any matches?

    You probably have too many preferences set. It's best to set only the really important stuff and allow Co-muter to find as many close matches as possible. Then you can review them and pick the one(s) that sound the best.

    And don't worry, we will will automatically try to match you up with anyone who signs up. If you don't see someone today, that could change really soon!

  • This is AMAZING! Can I get matches for my school too?

    We're working on it! Currently, the app is in beta testing and only works with St. Thomas Aquinas High School in Fort Lauderdale, FL.

    Send us your school name and we'll let you know as soon as Co-muter can match you there.
